Walk Hack Night II

Wednesday, February 8, 2017

Andrew Mondschein showed how UVA is using wearable sensors to measure dimensions of walkability in Tysons, VA and beyond

Walk Hack Night I

Thursday, August 20, 2015

James Graham will demo DDOT's VisionZero app
Stephanie Nguyen will demo the Landmark app for pedestrian-focused directions
Ted Eytan will show how the KP Center for Total Health uses infrared sensors and visualization tools to model trail use and promote walking in NoMA
